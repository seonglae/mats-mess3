\begin{abstract}
We train a small transformer on a non-ergodic dataset of Mess3 processes with different parameters, where each sequence comes from one ergodic component. The model must solve a hierarchical inference problem: identifying the component (meta-synchronization) and tracking belief states within it. We derive geometric predictions connecting this to the Factored World Hypothesis, verify Sierpinski gasket recovery ($R^2 = 0.989$), and analyze synchronization dynamics across positions and layers.
\end{abstract}
